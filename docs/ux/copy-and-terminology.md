# Copy & Terminology

## Key Terms

- "Brand" or "Business" → Clothing brand using the system.
- "Customer" → Person who calls or is called by the brand.
- "Call" → Phone call recorded by telephony provider.
- "Automation" → Automatic WhatsApp messages after calls.
- "WhatsApp Connection" → Settings to connect to WhatsApp Cloud API.
- "Telephony Connection" → Settings to connect to call provider.

## Labels & Button Text

- Login:
  - Title: "Sign in to your brand dashboard"
  - Button: "Login"

- Dashboard:
  - Section title: "Today at a glance"
  - Automation card title: "Post-call WhatsApp Automation"

- Setup Wizard:
  - Step 1 title: "Tell us about your brand"
  - Step 2 title: "Connect WhatsApp"
  - Step 3 title: "Connect your call provider"
  - Step 4 title: "Upload your product catalogue"
  - Step 5 title: "Turn on automation"

- Automation Settings:
  - Toggle label: "Enable post-call WhatsApp automation"
  - Help text: "We will automatically send a WhatsApp message after eligible calls."

## Status Labels

- Call statuses:
  - "Completed", "Missed", "Ringing", etc.
- Message statuses:
  - "Queued", "Sent", "Delivered", "Read", "Failed".

## Error Messages (Examples)

- WhatsApp connection failure:
  - "We couldn't connect to WhatsApp. Please check your Access Token and try again."
- Telephony credentials invalid:
  - "We couldn't verify your telephony credentials. Please check the values."
- CSV import error:
  - "Some rows in your file have errors. Please fix them and upload again."
